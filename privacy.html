Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Overview

Chip ("we", "our", or "us") is a bill-splitting app built for iPhone. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account information — your display name and Apple ID (via Sign in with Apple)
  • Payment handles — Venmo username, CashApp tag, Zelle phone number (optional, stored securely)
  • Expense data — group names, expense amounts, split information
  • Receipt images — processed by OpenAI's GPT-4o for item extraction (not stored permanently)
  • Device data — crash reports and performance data via Firebase Crashlytics

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide bill-splitting functionality across your groups
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase
  • To process AI receipt scanning via OpenAI
  • To manage your subscription via RevenueCat
  • To send push notifications about group activity
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and push notifications
  • OpenAI — AI receipt scanning (images are not stored)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, StoreKit payments

Each third party has its own privacy policy governing their data handling.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData and synced to Firebase Firestore. Receipt images are sent to OpenAI for processing and are not permanently stored on our servers.

Payment handles (Venmo, CashApp, Zelle) are stored in your account and used only to pre-fill payment deep links.

Bluetooth

Chip uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) for our Collect Mode feature, which allows nearby users to receive payment requests at a table. Bluetooth is only activated when you explicitly open Collect Mode and is not used for background tracking.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab. Upon deletion, your data is removed from Firebase within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Chip is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
